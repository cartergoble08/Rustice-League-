About Our Team

Rustice League (Team #26943) is a dedicated robotics team committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in competitive robotics. Our team comprises passionate students, mentors, and volunteers who work together to design, build, and program cutting-edge robots.


We believe in fostering a collaborative environment where creativity meets technical excellence. Through our participation in robotics competitions, we develop crucial STEM skills, leadership abilities, and problem-solving capabilities that prepare our members for future success.
